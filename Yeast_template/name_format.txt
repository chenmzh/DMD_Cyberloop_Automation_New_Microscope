Image usually with name:
1_brightfield_z1_t000001.tif
which is sample index, usually value from 1 to 24, then channels, here is brightfield, then z position, then time(such as t000001, t000002), end with .tif
frame rate is recorded in layout.xlsx, period table, with the unit of minutes.
Light intensity is saved in the same layout.xlsx. intensity table.
